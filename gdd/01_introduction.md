# Introduction

## Game Concept
A top-down 2D mining-themed game where players explore levels, extract resources from static asteroids, and build infrastructure to expand their reach.

## Vision Statement
To create an engaging and satisfying mining experience with a focus on progression, strategic base building, and power management.

## Target Platform
- Engine: Godot 4.2.2
- Platform: PC (initial target)
- Perspective: 2D Top-Down
